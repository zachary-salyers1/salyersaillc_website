Our Story

Founded with a vision to democratize artificial intelligence for businesses of all sizes, Salyers AI emerged from the recognition that every organization deserves access to cutting-edge AI solutions.

Based in Independence, Kentucky, we combine deep technical expertise with a practical understanding of business challenges. Our journey began with a simple belief: AI should enhance human potential, not replace it.

Today, we're proud to help businesses across various industries transform their operations through intelligent automation, predictive analytics, and innovative AI-driven solutions.
